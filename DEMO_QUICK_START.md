## 👥 Team Management Features

### Add Team Members
1. Go to **Team Setup** page
2. Fill in the form with responsibilities
3. Click **Add Member**

### Edit Team Members **NEW!**
- Just **click on any team member** in the list
- Edit modal opens with all their details
- Make changes and click **Save**

### Delete Team Members
- Click the **Remove** button next to their name
- Confirms before deleting

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

**Welcome modal not showing?**
- Just click the "🎯 Demo Setup" button!

**Can't edit team members?**
- Make sure you're clicking on the team member card itself (not the Remove button)

**Demo seems broken?**
- Click "🎯 Demo Setup" → "🔄 Clear All Data"
- Refresh the page
- Start fresh!
