## Summary
The finance team presented a revised framework for handling procurement and financial requests, centralizing all inquiries through a dedicated finance email address. ## Decisions
- Refine the financial services framework to improve clarity, predictability, fairness, and professional follow-up.
- Centralize all request handling through finance@mbzuai.ac.ae.
- Assign Aline and Kaltham as primary points of contact for all finance, procurement, and logistics requests.
- Adopt a Services Catalog to guide staff on request flows and processes.

## Risks
- [HIGH] Delays due to missing documentation or unclear approvals.

- [LOW] Potential overload of the finance email if not managed effectively.
